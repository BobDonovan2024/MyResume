Music Industry & Performance

  • Director / Producer, Affective Records UK - 2012 - Pressent

    After completing business management courses, Ian established Affective Records as a record company and independent label to
    provide songwriting/ arranging/ production/ Midi programming and mixing services. To date Ian has produced and released
    several studio albums by artists and worked and freelanced in several recording studios including Amazon/ Hard City/ Pearl in
    Liverpool and AudioHeart in Vienna. In addition Ian has also provided festival music consultancy, live music production and
    engineering.

  • Songwriter / Performer, IML Productions — 2006 - Present

    Ian set up IML Productions as a business platform for the promotion and development of his live/ music and art projects. Ian has
    performed both in the recording studio and live on TV, radio, festivals and international events in the USA and across Europe.

  • The Moptops Stage Musical — 1996-1998

    Drawing from his experiences, Ian wrote, directed and starred in his own theatre musical which successfully toured around
    the UK Experience.

  • "This Is Merseybeat" 2019 - 2024

    Performed as an actor and musician in the UK touring stage musical.

  • "Best" Liverpool Everyman — October-November 1995

    Ian co-starred in the Fred Lawless musical comedy portraying Paul McCartney acting/ singing and playing bass and guitar.

  • "Imagine" Liverpool Playhouse — May-August 1994

    Ian co-starred in the Bill Kenwright produced stage musical portraying Paul McCartney acting/ singing and playing
    bass/ guitar/ piano.
